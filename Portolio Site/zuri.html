Beginning an 8-week Journey with Zuri Internship Scholarship

Last month, I got a link to register for an 8-week internship in Frontend Web development withZuri. A few days ago, I got an email acknowleging my admission and a another link to formerly join its learning platform, hosted majorly on slack. Today, I am more than excited to have been admitted for the training and this article is part of the first assignments to move me to the next level - there are a total of 10 levels.

My major goal is to become a become an astute frontend web developer. Going forward, I am determined to not only attempt the series of assignments that would be given, I am poised to make the best use of whatevr resources are made available to interns. That is addition to personal efforts I am willing to put in to make the best of the training period and hone my skills in web development.

By the end of the training, I hope to have achieved the following:

  • Refine my knowledge of HTML, CSS & Javascript.
  • Hone my skills in basic Python Programming.
  • Learn Bootsrap, Django and Nodes.js which are frameworks for HTML, Python and Javascripts respectively.
  • Any other learning possible along the line.
